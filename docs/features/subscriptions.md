# Subscriptions

Access premium content and support Christ Gospel Church through subscriptions. Whether you are an individual listener or want to share with your family, there is a plan for you.

## Plan Tiers

The CGC platform offers multiple subscription tiers to fit your needs. Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) to view current plans and pricing.

### Free Tier

Everyone with a CGC account gets access to the free tier, which includes:

- Browse and stream sermons (audio and video)
- Search the sermon library
- Create personal playlists
- Access the AI-powered search
- Use the app in English or Spanish
- Receive push notifications for new content

### Premium (Individual) Plan

The Premium plan unlocks the full CGC experience for a single user:

- Everything in the Free tier, plus:
- **Offline downloads** — Download sermons, music, podcasts, and books for offline access
- **High-quality streaming** — Access higher audio and video quality options
- **Full media library access** — Unlimited access to music, podcasts, literature, and all premium content
- **Priority support** — Get faster responses from our support team
- **Ad-free experience** — Enjoy content without any interruptions

### Family Plan

The Family plan gives your household access to premium features at a better value:

- Everything in the Premium plan
- **Up to 5 accounts** — One primary account holder plus up to 4 family members
- **Individual profiles** — Each family member has their own account with personal playlists, preferences, and download history
- **Shared billing** — One subscription covers the entire family with a single payment

## Family Plan Details

The Family plan is designed for households that want to share a subscription.

### What is included

- One primary account holder and up to **4 additional family members** (5 total)
- Each person gets their own individual CGC account
- Each person has their own playlists, downloads, listening history, and preferences
- All members enjoy the same premium features

### How to set up a Family plan

1. Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org)
2. Select the **Family Plan**
3. Complete the checkout process with your payment details
4. After subscribing, go to **Settings > Subscription > Family Members** in the app or web dashboard
5. Tap **Invite Family Member**
6. Enter the email address of the family member you want to add
7. They will receive an email invitation to join your family plan
8. The family member needs their own CGC account — if they do not have one, they will be prompted to create one when they accept the invitation
9. Repeat for each family member (up to 4)

### Managing family members

- The primary account holder can add or remove family members at any time from **Settings > Subscription > Family Members**
- To remove a member, tap their name and select **Remove from Family Plan**
- Removed members will lose premium access at the end of the current billing period
- To replace a member, remove the existing one first, then invite the new person

::: tip
Each family member should use their own email address when creating their CGC account. This ensures they have their own personal experience with separate playlists and preferences.
:::

## Payment Methods

All payments are processed securely through **Stripe**, a trusted and PCI-compliant payment platform. Your card information is never stored on CGC servers.

### Accepted payment methods

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- Most major debit cards

### How to update your payment method

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription > Payment Method**
2. Click **Update Card**
3. Enter your new card details on the secure Stripe form
4. Click **Save**

Your new card will be used for all future payments. The change takes effect immediately.

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly billing

- You are billed once per month on the same date you originally subscribed
- For example, if you subscribed on March 15, you will be billed on the 15th of each month
- You can cancel at any time and keep access until the end of your current billing period

### Annual billing

- Pay once per year for a discounted rate compared to monthly billing
- Annual plans are billed on the anniversary of your subscription start date
- You save money compared to paying monthly — the annual discount is shown on the [subscriptions page](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org)

### Receipts

- A receipt is automatically sent to your email after each payment
- You can also view your full billing history in **Settings > Subscription > Billing History**
- Tap any payment entry to view or download the receipt

---

## What Happens When Your Subscription Expires

If your subscription is cancelled or your payment fails and is not updated:

1. **You keep access until the end of your current billing period** — Your premium features remain active until the paid period ends
2. **After expiration**, your account reverts to the Free tier:
   - You can still stream sermons and browse the library
   - Downloaded content becomes unavailable for offline playback (the files remain on your device but cannot be played until you resubscribe)
   - Access to premium-only content (full music library, literature, podcasts) may be restricted
3. **Your data is preserved** — Your playlists, preferences, and account information are not deleted. If you resubscribe later, everything will be restored
4. **Family members lose access** — If you are on a Family plan, all family members will revert to the Free tier when the subscription expires

::: info
If your payment fails, you will receive an email notification. Update your payment method promptly to avoid interruption. The system will automatically retry the charge a few times before the subscription is marked as expired.
:::

## Promo Codes and Coupons

From time to time, CGC may offer promo codes or coupons for discounted subscriptions.

### How to use a promo code

1. Go to [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org)
2. Select a plan and proceed to checkout
3. On the Stripe checkout page, look for the **Promo Code** or **Coupon** field
4. Enter your code exactly as provided (codes are case-sensitive)
5. Click **Apply** — you will see the discount reflected in your total
6. Complete the checkout

### Promo code guidelines

- Promo codes may have an **expiration date** — check the terms provided with the code
- Some codes apply only to specific plans (e.g., annual plan only)
- Most promo codes can only be used **once per account**
- Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers unless stated otherwise
- If a code is not working, double-check the spelling and expiration date, or contact **support@christgospel.org**

## Refund Policy

- Refund requests can be made within **7 days** of a payment
- Contact **support@christgospel.org** with your account email and the reason for the request
- Refunds are processed to your original payment method within **5-10 business days**
- If you cancel mid-cycle, you retain access until the period ends but the current period is not refunded
