# Subscriptions

Access premium content and support Christ Gospel Church through subscriptions.

## Plans

Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) to view available plans and pricing.

## Managing Your Subscription

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
3. From here you can:
   - View your current plan
   - Upgrade or downgrade
   - Update payment method
   - View billing history
   - Cancel your plan

## Payment

- Processed securely through **Stripe**
- Major credit and debit cards accepted
- Billing is automatic on your renewal date
- Receipts sent to your email

## Family Plans

Share your subscription with family members. Contact support for details on family plan options.
