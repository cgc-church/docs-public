# AI-Powered Features

The CGC platform uses artificial intelligence to help you discover content, find answers, and get personalized recommendations. These features are designed to enhance your experience and make it easier to engage with the church's library of sermons, music, and more.

## AI-Powered Sermon Search

Finding the right sermon has never been easier. Our AI-powered search goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand what you are looking for.

### How it works

- **Natural language search** — Instead of searching for exact titles, you can describe what you are looking for in everyday language. For example, search for "sermons about overcoming fear" or "messages on faith during difficult times."
- **Contextual understanding** — The AI understands the meaning behind your words, not just the words themselves. It can find relevant sermons even if your exact search terms do not appear in the title or description.
- **Topic recognition** — The search engine recognizes biblical themes, scripture references, and common topics, so you can search by theme and get accurate results.

### Tips for better search results

- Be descriptive — "How to strengthen your prayer life" will give better results than just "prayer"
- Include context — Mentioning a topic, scripture, or theme helps the AI narrow down the most relevant content
- Try different phrasing — If your first search does not return what you expected, try rephrasing your query

## AI Chat Assistant

The AI Chat Assistant is a conversational tool built into the CGC app that can help you find content, answer questions, and guide you through the platform.

### What the AI assistant can do

- **Answer questions about content** — Ask things like "What sermons are available about the Book of Revelation?" or "Show me recent sermons from this month."
- **Help you navigate** — If you are not sure where to find something, ask the assistant. For example, "How do I download a sermon?" or "Where can I update my payment method?"
- **Provide scripture context** — Ask about a scripture passage and the assistant can help you find related sermons and content in the library.
- **Summarize content** — Get a brief overview of what a sermon covers before you listen to it.

### How to use the AI assistant

1. Open the CGC app
2. Tap the **chat** icon (usually found in the bottom navigation or as a floating button)
3. Type your question or request in natural language
4. The assistant will respond with helpful information, links, and suggestions

::: tip
The AI assistant is designed to help you find and enjoy content on the CGC platform. For account or billing issues, please contact **support@christgospel.org** for personalized help.
:::

## Smart Recommendations

The CGC platform learns from your listening habits to suggest content you might enjoy.

### How recommendations work

- **Based on your history** — The more you listen, the better the recommendations become. The platform notes which preachers, topics, and series you enjoy and suggests similar content.
- **Based on popular content** — Sermons and songs that are popular with the community may be surfaced as recommendations, especially for newer users.
- **Weekly picks** — Each week, the platform highlights recommended sermons and music based on trending content and your personal interests.
- **Related content** — When you are listening to or viewing a sermon, you will see "Related Sermons" or "You Might Also Enjoy" suggestions nearby.

### Where to find recommendations

- **Home screen** — Personalized recommendations appear on the app's home screen
- **After playback** — When a sermon or song finishes, you will see suggestions for what to listen to next
- **Explore section** — Browse curated recommendations organized by topic, preacher, or theme

## How AI Enhances Your Experience

All of the AI features in the CGC platform are designed with one goal: to help you connect with the content that matters most to you. Here is a summary of how AI makes the platform better:

| Feature | What it does for you |
|---|---|
| **AI Search** | Finds sermons using natural language, so you do not need to know exact titles or keywords |
| **AI Chat Assistant** | Answers your questions and helps you navigate the platform in a conversational way |
| **Smart Recommendations** | Suggests sermons, music, and content tailored to your interests and listening history |
| **Related Content** | Shows you similar sermons and media so you can keep exploring topics you care about |

### Privacy and AI

Your privacy is important to us. Here is how we handle your data with respect to AI features:

- Recommendation data is used only to improve your personal experience on the CGC platform
- Your listening history is kept private and is not shared with other users
- You can clear your listening history at any time in **Settings > Privacy**
- AI features can be used without a subscription, though some content they recommend may require premium access
