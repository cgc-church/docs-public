# Getting Started

Welcome to the Christ Gospel Church app! Whether you want to watch sermons, read devotionals, or stay connected with your church family, this app puts it all at your fingertips.

## What You Can Do

- Stream sermons and teachings on demand
- Browse and read devotional content
- Set up a personal profile to save your favorites
- Join a family plan to share access with loved ones
- Receive notifications about new content and events
- Access your library of saved messages anytime

## How It Works

**Scenario: First-time visitor explores the app**
- **Given** I have just downloaded the CGC app
- **When** I open the app for the first time
- **Then** I see a welcome screen with options to create an account or log in

**Scenario: Returning member logs in**
- **Given** I already have a CGC account
- **When** I open the app and tap "Log In"
- **Then** I can enter my email and password to access my account

**Scenario: New member creates an account**
- **Given** I am on the welcome screen
- **When** I tap "Create Account"
- **Then** I am guided through a simple registration process

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I don't know if I already have an account | Try logging in with your email. If no account exists, you'll be prompted to create one. |
| The app won't open after downloading | Make sure your device meets the minimum requirements (iOS 15+ or Android 10+) and restart your device. |
| I'm not sure which features are free | All core content is available with a free account. Some premium features may require a subscription. |
