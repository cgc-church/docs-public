# Create Your Account

Setting up a CGC account is quick and easy. Once you have an account, you can save your favorite sermons, manage your profile, and access all the features the app has to offer.

## What You Can Do

- Create a new CGC account using your email address
- Verify your email to activate your account
- Set up your personal profile with your name and photo
- Start using the app right away after registration

## How It Works

**Scenario: Starting the registration process**
- **Given** I have the CGC app open on my device
- **When** I tap "Create Account" on the welcome screen
- **Then** I see the first step of the registration form

**Scenario: Completing registration with valid information**
- **Given** I am on the registration screen
- **When** I fill in my name, email, and a password that meets the requirements
- **Then** my account is created and I receive a verification email

**Scenario: Verifying my email address**
- **Given** I have just created my account
- **When** I open the verification email and tap the confirmation link
- **Then** my email is verified and my account is fully activated

**Scenario: Trying to register with an email that already has an account**
- **Given** I am on the registration screen
- **When** I enter an email address that is already tied to an existing account
- **Then** I see a message letting me know the email is already in use, with an option to log in instead

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I never received the verification email | Check your spam or junk folder. If it's not there, go back to the app and tap "Resend Verification Email." |
| The email says "this email is already in use" | You may already have an account. Try logging in with that email, or use the "Forgot Password" option if you don't remember your password. |
| My password doesn't meet the requirements | Make sure your password is at least 8 characters and includes both letters and numbers. Avoid using common words or simple patterns. |
| I made a typo in my email during registration | If you haven't verified yet, create a new account with the correct email. If you already verified, contact support to update your email. |
| The app crashes during registration | Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. Try closing and reopening the app, then start the registration again. |
