# Download the App

The CGC app is available for both iPhone and Android devices. Follow the steps below to get started.

## What You Can Do

- Download the CGC app from the Apple App Store (iPhone and iPad)
- Download the CGC app from Google Play (Android phones and tablets)
- Verify your device meets the minimum requirements before installing

## Device Requirements

Before downloading, make sure your device is compatible:

- **iPhone or iPad:** iOS 15 or later
- **Android phone or tablet:** Android 10 or later

Not sure which version you're running? Check your device settings under "About Phone" or "About This iPhone" to find your operating system version.

## How It Works

**Scenario: Downloading on an iPhone**
- **Given** I am using an iPhone with iOS 15 or later
- **When** I open the App Store and search for "CGC" or "Christ Gospel Church"
- **Then** I see the CGC app and can tap "Get" to download it

**Scenario: Downloading on an Android device**
- **Given** I am using an Android phone with Android 10 or later
- **When** I open Google Play and search for "CGC" or "Christ Gospel Church"
- **Then** I see the CGC app and can tap "Install" to download it

**Scenario: Device does not meet requirements**
- **Given** I am using a device with an older operating system
- **When** I try to download the CGC app
- **Then** I see a message that my device is not compatible and I need to update my operating system

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't find the app in the store | Make sure you're searching for "CGC" or "Christ Gospel Church." Check your spelling and try again. |
| The app says my device is not compatible | Your device may need a software update. Go to your device settings and check for available updates. |
| The download seems stuck | Make sure you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi recommended). Try pausing and resuming the download. |
| I don't have enough storage space | Free up space on your device by removing unused apps or photos, then try downloading again. |
| I see multiple apps with similar names | Look for the official CGC app with the Christ Gospel Church logo. |
