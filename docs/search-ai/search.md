# Search

## What You Can Do
- Search across all content types at once (sermons, music, books, videos, podcasts)
- Use filters to narrow results by content type, date, speaker, or topic
- View and revisit your recent search history
- Get AI-powered search suggestions as you type
- Find specific passages, quotes, or topics across the entire library

## How It Works

**Scenario: Searching for a sermon topic**
- **Given** I am on the Search screen
- **When** I type "forgiveness" in the search bar and tap search
- **Then** I see results from sermons, books, and other content related to forgiveness

**Scenario: Filtering search results**
- **Given** I have performed a search and see results
- **When** I tap the "Sermons" filter tab
- **Then** only sermon results are displayed, hiding other content types

**Scenario: Using search history**
- **Given** I am on the Search screen
- **When** I tap the search bar without typing anything
- **Then** I see my recent searches and can tap any of them to search again

**Scenario: Getting AI-powered suggestions**
- **Given** I am typing in the search bar
- **When** I begin typing a word or phrase
- **Then** the app suggests relevant search terms and content matches below the search bar

**Scenario: Clearing search history**
- **Given** I am viewing my recent searches
- **When** I tap "Clear History"
- **Then** all my past searches are removed from the list

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Performing a Search
1. Tap the **Search** tab at the bottom of the app
2. Tap the **search bar** at the top of the screen
3. Type your search term (a topic, speaker name, song title, scripture reference, etc.)
4. Tap **Search** on your keyboard
5. Browse the results, which are organized by content type

### Filtering Results
1. After performing a search, view the results on screen
2. Tap the **filter tabs** at the top of the results (All, Sermons, Music, Books, Podcasts, Videos)
3. Select a tab to show only that content type
4. To narrow further, tap the **Filter** icon and choose additional options like date range, speaker, or topic
5. Tap **Apply** to see filtered results

### Using Search History
1. Tap the **Search** tab
2. Tap the **search bar**
3. Your recent searches appear below the bar
4. Tap any previous search to run it again
5. To remove a single search, swipe left on it; to clear all, tap **Clear History**

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| No results found | Try using different keywords or a broader search term. Check for typos. |
| Results seem unrelated to my search | Use the filter tabs to narrow by content type. Try more specific search terms. |
| My search history isn't showing | Make sure you're logged in. Search history is saved to your account. |
| Search is slow or unresponsive | Check your internet connection. Search requires an active connection. |
| I can't find a specific sermon I know exists | Try searching by the speaker's name, the exact sermon title, or a key phrase from the sermon. |
| AI suggestions aren't appearing | Ensure you have a stable internet connection. AI-powered suggestions require connectivity. |
