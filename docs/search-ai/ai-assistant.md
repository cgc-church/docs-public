# AI Assistant

## What You Can Do
- Ask questions about faith, scripture, sermons, and church content
- Use **Ask Mode** to get direct answers to your questions
- Use **Agent Mode** for guided discovery that helps you explore topics step by step
- Use **Devotional Mode** to receive personalized daily devotionals and reflections
- Speak your questions using voice input
- Track and manage your AI credits

## How It Works

**Scenario: Asking a question in Ask Mode**
- **Given** I am on the AI Assistant screen in Ask Mode
- **When** I type "What does the Bible say about patience?" and tap send
- **Then** the AI provides a thoughtful answer with relevant scripture references and links to related sermons

**Scenario: Exploring a topic in Agent Mode**
- **Given** I have switched to Agent Mode
- **When** I type "I want to learn about prayer"
- **Then** the AI guides me through a series of follow-up questions and suggestions, helping me discover sermons, books, and content about prayer

**Scenario: Getting a devotional in Devotional Mode**
- **Given** I have switched to Devotional Mode
- **When** I open the AI Assistant
- **Then** the AI presents a personalized daily devotional with a scripture passage, reflection, and prayer prompt

**Scenario: Using voice input**
- **Given** I am on the AI Assistant screen
- **When** I tap the microphone icon and speak my question
- **Then** my voice is transcribed to text and the AI responds as if I had typed it

**Scenario: Running out of AI credits**
- **Given** I have used all my available AI credits
- **When** I try to send a message to the AI
- **Then** I see a message letting me know I'm out of credits, with an option to purchase more

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Starting a Conversation in Ask Mode
1. Tap the **AI Assistant** icon in the app (usually in the bottom navigation or Search section)
2. Make sure **Ask Mode** is selected at the top
3. Type your question in the message bar at the bottom
4. Tap **Send**
5. Read the AI's response, which may include scripture references and links to related content

### Switching to Agent Mode
1. Open the **AI Assistant**
2. Tap the **mode selector** at the top of the screen
3. Select **Agent Mode**
4. Type a topic you want to explore (for example, "Help me understand the book of Romans")
5. Follow the AI's guided prompts to go deeper into the topic

### Using Devotional Mode
1. Open the **AI Assistant**
2. Tap the **mode selector** at the top of the screen
3. Select **Devotional Mode**
4. The AI presents a daily devotional tailored to you
5. Read the scripture passage, reflection, and prayer prompt
6. Optionally, type a response or follow-up question to continue the conversation

### Using Voice Input
1. Open the **AI Assistant** in any mode
2. Tap the **microphone icon** next to the message bar
3. Speak your question clearly
4. Your speech is converted to text automatically
5. Review the text and tap **Send**, or tap the microphone again to re-record

### Checking Your AI Credits
1. Open the **AI Assistant**
2. Tap the **credits indicator** (usually shown at the top of the screen)
3. View your remaining credits and usage history
4. If you need more credits, tap **Get More Credits** to purchase a credit pack

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| The AI gave an inaccurate answer | AI responses are generated based on available content and may not always be perfect. Always verify scripture references with your Bible. |
| I'm out of AI credits | You can purchase additional credit packs from the AI Assistant screen or from your Subscriptions settings. Some plans include monthly credits. |
| Voice input isn't working | Make sure you've granted the app permission to access your microphone. Check that your device's microphone is not muted. |
| The AI doesn't understand my question | Try rephrasing your question using simpler or more specific language. |
| Devotional Mode shows the same content | Devotionals refresh daily. Check back tomorrow for a new devotional. You can also ask follow-up questions to continue the conversation. |
| I can't find the AI Assistant | The AI Assistant may be located under the Search tab or in the main navigation. Check your app's bottom menu. |
