# Notes & Highlights

## What You Can Do
- Highlight text in books and sermon transcripts with different colors
- Create personal notes attached to specific passages
- Organize notes into notebooks for different topics or studies
- Add tags to notes for easy searching
- View all your highlights and notes in one central place
- Export or review your notes anytime

## How It Works

**Scenario: Highlighting text in a book**
- **Given** I am reading a book or sermon transcript
- **When** I press and hold on a word, then drag to select a passage
- **Then** a highlight toolbar appears and I can choose a highlight color

**Scenario: Adding a note to a highlight**
- **Given** I have just highlighted a passage
- **When** I tap "Add Note" on the highlight toolbar
- **Then** a text field opens where I can type my personal note and save it

**Scenario: Organizing notes into a notebook**
- **Given** I am viewing my Notes & Highlights screen
- **When** I select a note and tap "Move to Notebook"
- **Then** I can choose an existing notebook or create a new one to organize my notes

**Scenario: Viewing all highlights for a book**
- **Given** I am reading a book
- **When** I tap the "Notes & Highlights" icon in the reading toolbar
- **Then** I see a list of all my highlights and notes for that book

**Scenario: Searching notes by tag**
- **Given** I am on the Notes & Highlights screen in My Library
- **When** I tap a tag I previously created
- **Then** only notes with that tag are displayed

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Highlighting Text
1. Open a book or sermon transcript
2. Press and hold on the starting word of the passage you want to highlight
3. Drag the selection handles to cover the full passage
4. A toolbar appears above the selection
5. Tap the **highlight color** you prefer
6. The text is now highlighted and saved automatically

### Adding a Note
1. Highlight a passage as described above
2. Tap **Add Note** on the toolbar
3. Type your note in the text field
4. Optionally, add a **tag** (such as "prayer," "study," or any word you choose)
5. Tap **Save**

### Creating and Using Notebooks
1. Go to **My Library** and tap **Notes & Highlights**
2. Tap **Notebooks** at the top
3. Tap **New Notebook** and give it a name (for example, "Sunday Sermons" or "Bible Study")
4. To move a note into a notebook, select the note, tap the **three-dot menu**, and choose **Move to Notebook**
5. Select the notebook and tap **Move**

### Viewing All Your Notes and Highlights
1. Go to **My Library**
2. Tap **Notes & Highlights**
3. Browse all notes and highlights across all content
4. Use the **filter** options to narrow by book, notebook, or tag

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't select text to highlight | Make sure you're pressing and holding long enough. Some content types (like audio-only sermons) don't support highlighting. |
| My highlights disappeared | Check that you're logged in to the same account. Highlights sync to your profile. |
| I can't find a note I created | Use the search bar or filter by tags on the Notes & Highlights screen. Also check your notebooks. |
| The highlight toolbar doesn't appear | Try selecting a smaller passage first. If the issue persists, close and reopen the content. |
| I want to change a highlight color | Tap on the existing highlight, then select a new color from the toolbar. |
| I accidentally deleted a note | Unfortunately, deleted notes cannot be recovered. Consider creating a backup by taking a screenshot of important notes. |
