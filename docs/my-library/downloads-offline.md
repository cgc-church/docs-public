# Downloads & Offline Access

## What You Can Do
- Download sermons, music, books, and podcasts for offline listening or reading
- Access downloaded content without an internet connection
- Manage your downloads and free up device storage
- Delete individual downloads or clear all at once
- Automatically sync your progress when you reconnect to the internet

## How It Works

**Scenario: Downloading a sermon for offline use**
- **Given** I am viewing a sermon I want to save
- **When** I tap the download icon
- **Then** the sermon begins downloading and a progress indicator shows the status

**Scenario: Accessing downloaded content offline**
- **Given** I have downloaded content and I am without internet access
- **When** I go to My Library and tap "Downloads"
- **Then** I see all my downloaded content and can play or read it without a connection

**Scenario: Deleting a single download**
- **Given** I am viewing my Downloads list
- **When** I swipe left on an item and tap "Delete"
- **Then** the downloaded file is removed from my device, freeing up storage

**Scenario: Checking download storage usage**
- **Given** I want to see how much space my downloads are using
- **When** I go to My Library, then Downloads, then tap "Manage Storage"
- **Then** I see a breakdown of storage used by content type

**Scenario: Auto-syncing after going back online**
- **Given** I listened to downloaded content while offline
- **When** I reconnect to the internet
- **Then** my playback progress, notes, and favorites automatically sync to my account

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Downloading Content
1. Find the sermon, song, album, book, or podcast you want to download
2. Tap the **download icon** (downward arrow) next to the item
3. A progress bar shows the download status
4. Once complete, the icon changes to a checkmark indicating the content is saved
5. The item now appears in your **Downloads** section

### Accessing Downloads Offline
1. Open the app (no internet needed)
2. Go to **My Library**
3. Tap **Downloads**
4. Browse and play any downloaded content
5. Your progress is saved locally and will sync when you're back online

### Managing Storage
1. Go to **My Library**
2. Tap **Downloads**
3. Tap **Manage Storage** at the top
4. View a breakdown of how much space each content type is using
5. Choose to delete items individually or by category to free up space
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->

### Deleting a Download
1. Go to **My Library** and tap **Downloads**
2. Find the item you want to remove
3. Swipe left on the item (or tap the three-dot menu)
4. Tap **Delete Download**
5. The file is removed from your device (it's still available to stream online)
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->

### Clearing All Downloads
1. Go to **My Library** and tap **Downloads**
2. Tap **Manage Storage**
3. Tap **Clear All Downloads**
4. Confirm by tapping **Delete All**
5. All downloaded files are removed from your device

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| A download is stuck or won't complete | Check your internet connection and available storage space. Try canceling the download and starting it again. |
| I can't find my downloaded content | Go to My Library and tap Downloads. Make sure you're logged in to the correct account. |
| My device is running low on storage | Go to Downloads, then Manage Storage, and delete content you no longer need offline. |
| My progress didn't sync after going online | Make sure the app is open and connected to the internet. Pull down to refresh and trigger a sync. |
| Downloaded content won't play | The file may be corrupted. Delete the download and re-download the content. |
| I can't download certain content | Some content may not be available for download depending on your subscription plan. Check your plan details. |
