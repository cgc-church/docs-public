# Playlists

## What You Can Do
- Create custom playlists with your favorite songs and sermons
- Add content from anywhere in the app to a playlist
- Reorder items within a playlist using drag-and-drop
- Rename or delete playlists you no longer need
- Browse featured playlists curated by the church
- Share playlists with friends and family

## How It Works

**Scenario: Creating a new playlist**
- **Given** I am on the My Library screen
- **When** I tap "New Playlist" and enter a name
- **Then** an empty playlist is created and ready for me to add content

**Scenario: Adding a song to a playlist**
- **Given** I am listening to a song or viewing a sermon
- **When** I tap the three-dot menu and select "Add to Playlist"
- **Then** I can choose an existing playlist or create a new one, and the item is added

**Scenario: Reordering items in a playlist**
- **Given** I am viewing a playlist I created
- **When** I tap "Edit" and drag an item to a new position
- **Then** the playlist order updates to reflect my changes

**Scenario: Browsing featured playlists**
- **Given** I am on the Playlists section of My Library
- **When** I scroll to the "Featured" section
- **Then** I see playlists curated by the church that I can browse and follow

**Scenario: Deleting a playlist**
- **Given** I am viewing one of my playlists
- **When** I tap the three-dot menu and select "Delete Playlist"
- **Then** the playlist is removed from my library (the original content is not deleted)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Creating a Playlist
1. Open the app and go to **My Library**
2. Tap the **Playlists** tab
3. Tap **New Playlist**
4. Enter a name for your playlist
5. Tap **Create**

### Adding Content to a Playlist
1. Find a song, sermon, or other content you want to add
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) next to the item
3. Select **Add to Playlist**
4. Choose an existing playlist or tap **New Playlist** to create one
5. The item is added and a confirmation message appears
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->

### Reordering Playlist Items
1. Open the playlist you want to edit
2. Tap **Edit** in the top-right corner
3. Press and hold the drag handle next to any item
4. Drag the item up or down to its new position
5. Tap **Done** when finished

### Deleting a Playlist
1. Open the playlist you want to remove
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) at the top
3. Select **Delete Playlist**
4. Confirm by tapping **Delete**

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't find my playlist | Check that you're logged in to the correct account. Playlists are tied to your profile. |
| I can't reorder items | Make sure you tap "Edit" first. Drag-and-drop only works in edit mode. |
| A song I added is missing | The original content may have been removed from the library. Try searching for it again. |
| I don't see featured playlists | Featured playlists require an internet connection. Check your network and pull down to refresh. |
| I accidentally deleted a playlist | Unfortunately, deleted playlists cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new one. |
