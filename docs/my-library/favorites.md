# Favorites

## What You Can Do
- Add sermons, songs, books, podcasts, and videos to your favorites
- View all your favorited items in one place
- Remove items from your favorites at any time
- Filter your favorites by content type
- Access your favorites across all your devices

## How It Works

**Scenario: Adding a sermon to favorites**
- **Given** I am watching or browsing a sermon
- **When** I tap the heart icon
- **Then** the sermon is saved to my Favorites list and the heart icon fills in

**Scenario: Adding a song to favorites**
- **Given** I am listening to a song or browsing a music album
- **When** I tap the heart icon next to the song
- **Then** the song appears in my Favorites under the Music section

**Scenario: Viewing all favorites**
- **Given** I am on the My Library screen
- **When** I tap "Favorites"
- **Then** I see all my favorited content organized by type (sermons, music, books, podcasts)

**Scenario: Removing an item from favorites**
- **Given** I am viewing my Favorites list
- **When** I tap the filled heart icon on an item
- **Then** the item is removed from my favorites

**Scenario: Filtering favorites by type**
- **Given** I am viewing my Favorites list
- **When** I tap a filter such as "Music" or "Sermons"
- **Then** only favorites of that type are shown

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Adding an Item to Favorites
1. Browse or search for the content you enjoy
2. Tap the **heart icon** next to the item's title
3. The heart fills in, confirming the item has been added to your favorites

### Viewing Your Favorites
1. Open the app and go to **My Library**
2. Tap **Favorites**
3. Browse all your saved items
4. Use the filter tabs at the top to narrow by content type (Sermons, Music, Books, Podcasts)

### Removing an Item from Favorites
1. Go to **My Library** and tap **Favorites**
2. Find the item you want to remove
3. Tap the **filled heart icon** next to the item
4. The item is removed from your favorites list

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| My favorites aren't showing on another device | Make sure you're signed in with the same account on both devices. Favorites sync automatically when you're online. |
| The heart icon isn't responding | Check your internet connection. Favorites require a brief connection to save. |
| I accidentally removed a favorite | Simply find the content again and tap the heart icon to re-add it. |
| I have too many favorites to browse | Use the filter tabs at the top of the Favorites screen to narrow results by content type. |
| A favorited item shows as unavailable | The content may have been temporarily removed or updated. Try refreshing the page. |
