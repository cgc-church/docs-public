# Your Profile

Your profile is where you manage your personal information in the CGC app. You can update your name, change your profile photo, update your email address, and change your password at any time.

## What You Can Do

- View your current profile information
- Change your first or last name
- Add or update your profile photo
- Update your email address
- Change your password from within the app

## How It Works

**Scenario: Viewing my profile**
- **Given** I am logged in to the CGC app
- **When** I tap on my profile icon or navigate to the Profile section
- **Then** I see my current name, email, and profile photo

**Scenario: Updating my name**
- **Given** I am on the Profile screen
- **When** I tap on my name, update it, and tap "Save"
- **Then** my name is updated across the app

**Scenario: Changing my profile photo**
- **Given** I am on the Profile screen
- **When** I tap on my profile photo and select a new image from my device
- **Then** my profile photo is updated

**Scenario: Changing my password from the profile screen**
- **Given** I am on the Profile screen
- **When** I tap "Change Password," enter my current password, and enter a new password
- **Then** my password is updated and I can use the new password next time I log in

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I can't find the Profile section | Look for a person icon in the bottom navigation bar. You can also access it from the app menu. |
| My photo won't upload | Make sure the image is not too large. Try using a smaller photo or taking a new one directly from the app. |
| I changed my email but didn't get a verification email | Check your spam or junk folder at the new email address. If you don't see it, try updating your email again. |
| I don't remember my current password (needed to change it) | Use the "Forgot Password?" option on the login screen instead. See [Password Recovery](password-recovery.md). |
| My name change isn't showing up | Try closing and reopening the app. The update may take a moment to appear everywhere. |
