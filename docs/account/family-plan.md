# Family Plan

A family plan lets you share your CGC subscription with your household. One person sets up the plan, and family members join using a simple invite code. Everyone on the plan gets their own account with their own saved content.

## What You Can Do

- Join a family plan by entering an invite code
- Share your subscription benefits with up to 6 family members
- Each family member keeps their own separate profile and saved content
- View who is part of your family plan
- Leave a family plan at any time

## Benefits of a Family Plan

- **Shared subscription** — Everyone on the plan enjoys the same subscription benefits without paying separately.
- **Individual accounts** — Each person has their own login, profile, and saved library. Your content stays private to you.
- **Easy setup** — Joining is as simple as entering a short invite code. No extra downloads or complicated steps.
- **Manage from one place** — The plan owner can see who's on the plan and manage members easily.

## How It Works

**Scenario: Joining a family plan with an invite code**
- **Given** I have a CGC account and I've received an invite code from a family member
- **When** I go to Settings, tap "Family Plan," and enter the invite code
- **Then** I am added to the family plan and gain access to shared subscription benefits

**Scenario: Viewing family plan members**
- **Given** I am part of a family plan
- **When** I go to Settings and tap "Family Plan"
- **Then** I see a list of all members on the plan

**Scenario: Leaving a family plan**
- **Given** I am a member of a family plan (but not the owner)
- **When** I go to Settings, tap "Family Plan," and tap "Leave Plan"
- **Then** I am removed from the plan and lose access to shared subscription benefits

**Scenario: The plan owner removes a member**
- **Given** I am the owner of a family plan
- **When** I go to the Family Plan screen and tap "Remove" next to a member's name
- **Then** that member is removed from the plan

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| My invite code isn't working | Make sure you're entering the code exactly as it was shared, including any uppercase letters or numbers. Codes are case-sensitive. |
| I don't see the "Family Plan" option in Settings | Make sure your app is updated to the latest version. Family plan features may not be available on older versions. |
| I left a family plan and lost access to content | Leaving a plan removes your access to shared subscription benefits. Your personal saved content is still available, but premium features may require your own subscription. |
| The plan says it's full | Family plans support up to 6 members. The plan owner will need to remove a member before someone new can join. |
| I want to switch to a different family plan | You'll need to leave your current plan first, then join the new plan using a new invite code. |
