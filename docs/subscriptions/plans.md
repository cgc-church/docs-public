# Subscription Plans

## What You Can Do
- Browse available subscription plans
- Compare free and premium features side by side
- Choose the plan that best fits your needs
- Access exclusive content and features with a premium subscription

## How It Works

**Scenario: Viewing available plans**
- **Given** I am on the Subscriptions screen
- **When** I tap "View Plans"
- **Then** I see all available subscription plans with pricing and features listed

**Scenario: Comparing free vs premium features**
- **Given** I am viewing the plans comparison
- **When** I review the feature list
- **Then** I can clearly see which features are included in the free plan and which require a premium subscription

**Scenario: Selecting a plan to subscribe**
- **Given** I am viewing the available plans
- **When** I tap "Subscribe" on my chosen plan
- **Then** I am taken to the payment screen to complete my subscription

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Viewing Available Plans
1. Open the app and go to **Settings** or **Account**
2. Tap **Subscription** or **Plans**
3. Browse the available plans with their pricing
4. Review the features included in each plan

### Choosing a Plan
1. On the plans screen, compare the options
2. Consider which features matter most to you (offline downloads, AI access, full library, etc.)
3. Tap **Subscribe** on the plan you want
4. Follow the prompts to complete payment
5. Your new plan takes effect immediately

### Understanding Your Current Plan
1. Go to **Settings** and tap **Subscription**
2. Your current plan is shown at the top with a label such as "Free" or "Premium"
3. Below your plan name, you can see your renewal date and included features

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I'm not sure which plan is right for me | Start with the free plan to explore the app. You can upgrade anytime if you want offline access, the full library, or AI features. |
| I don't see a subscribe button | Make sure you're logged in. If you already have a subscription, the screen will show your current plan instead. |
| The price shown doesn't match what I expected | Pricing may vary by region or promotional offers. The final price is confirmed on the payment screen before you are charged. |
| I subscribed but features aren't unlocked | Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, go to Settings, then Subscription, and tap "Restore Purchases." |
| I want to change my plan | See the Manage Subscription help page for steps to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan. |
