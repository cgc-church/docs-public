# AI Credits

## What You Can Do
- Understand what AI credits are and how they work
- Check your current credit balance at any time
- Purchase additional credit packs when you need more
- See how credits are consumed by different AI features
- Track your credit usage history

## How It Works

**Scenario: Checking my credit balance**
- **Given** I am using the app
- **When** I open the AI Assistant or go to my Subscription settings
- **Then** I see my remaining AI credits displayed clearly

**Scenario: Using credits in a conversation**
- **Given** I have AI credits available
- **When** I send a message to the AI Assistant
- **Then** a small number of credits are used for the response, and my balance updates

**Scenario: Purchasing more credits**
- **Given** I am running low on credits or have run out
- **When** I tap "Get More Credits" from the AI Assistant or Subscription settings
- **Then** I see available credit packs with pricing and can complete a purchase

**Scenario: Receiving monthly credits with my plan**
- **Given** I have a premium subscription that includes monthly AI credits
- **When** my billing cycle renews
- **Then** my AI credits are replenished with the amount included in my plan

**Scenario: Running out of credits**
- **Given** I have used all my AI credits
- **When** I try to send a message in the AI Assistant
- **Then** I see a notice that I'm out of credits with an option to purchase more or wait until my next renewal

## Understanding AI Credits

AI credits are the currency used to interact with the AI Assistant. Each time you ask a question, request a devotional, or explore a topic with the AI, a small number of credits are used. The number of credits consumed depends on the complexity of your request:

- **Simple questions** (like asking for a scripture reference) use fewer credits
- **Detailed responses** (like exploring a topic in Agent Mode) use more credits
- **Devotional Mode** reflections use a moderate number of credits
- **Voice input** uses the same credits as typed input

Premium subscribers receive a monthly allowance of AI credits that refresh with each billing cycle. If you need more, you can purchase additional credit packs at any time.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Checking Your Credit Balance
1. Open the **AI Assistant** screen
2. Look for the **credit balance** indicator at the top of the screen
3. The number displayed is your remaining credits
4. Alternatively, go to **Settings**, then **Subscription**, and view your credits there

### Purchasing Additional Credits
1. Open the **AI Assistant** or go to **Settings** and tap **Subscription**
2. Tap **Get More Credits** or **Buy Credits**
3. Browse the available credit packs (various quantities and prices)
4. Select the pack you want
5. Confirm the purchase through your payment method
6. Credits are added to your balance immediately

### Viewing Credit Usage History
1. Go to **Settings** and tap **Subscription**
2. Tap **AI Credits** or **Credit History**
3. View a list of your recent credit usage, showing the date, activity, and credits consumed
4. This helps you understand how quickly you use credits and plan accordingly

### Understanding Monthly Credit Renewal
1. If you have a premium plan, your credits are included in your subscription
2. Go to **Settings**, then **Subscription** to see when your credits renew
3. Your renewal date is the same as your subscription billing date
4. Unused credits from the previous month may not carry over (check your plan details)

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| I ran out of credits | Purchase an additional credit pack from the AI Assistant screen or Subscription settings. Premium subscribers can also wait for their monthly renewal. |
| I don't know how many credits I have | Check the credit balance indicator at the top of the AI Assistant screen or in your Subscription settings. |
| I was charged for credits I didn't use | Credits are consumed per AI interaction. Review your credit history in Subscription settings to see how they were used. If you believe there's an error, contact support. |
| My monthly credits didn't refresh | Credits renew on your billing date. Check your renewal date in Subscription settings. If your billing date has passed and credits haven't refreshed, try restarting the app or contact support. |
| I purchased credits but they didn't appear | Close and reopen the app. If credits still don't show, go to Subscription settings and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue continues, contact support with your purchase receipt. |
| I'm not sure which credit pack to buy | Start with a smaller pack to see how quickly you use credits. You can always purchase more later. |
