# Themes

## What You Can Do
- Browse over 15 visual themes for the app
- Preview how a theme looks before applying it
- Switch between light, dark, and specialty themes at any time
- Choose themes designed for specific groups such as kids, youth, or senoritas

## How It Works

**Scenario: Browsing available themes**
- **Given** I am on the Settings screen
- **When** I tap **Themes**
- **Then** I see a gallery of available themes with preview thumbnails

**Scenario: Previewing a theme**
- **Given** I am on the Themes screen
- **When** I tap on a theme thumbnail
- **Then** I see a live preview of how the app will look with that theme applied

**Scenario: Applying a theme**
- **Given** I am previewing a theme
- **When** I tap **Apply**
- **Then** the app immediately updates to use the selected theme

**Scenario: Switching back to the default theme**
- **Given** I have a custom theme applied
- **When** I go to Settings, tap **Themes**, and select **Light**
- **Then** the app returns to the default light theme

## Available Themes

The CGC app includes a wide variety of themes to suit your style and preference:

- **Light** -- The default clean, bright look
- **Dark** -- A dark background for easier reading in low light
- **Dark Blue** -- A deep blue tone for a refined feel
- **Rose** -- Soft pink tones for a warm appearance
- **Purple** -- Rich purple accents throughout the app
- **Kids** -- Fun, colorful design made for younger users
- **Youth** -- A modern theme designed for teen and young adult groups
- **Senoritas** -- A custom theme for the Senoritas ministry group
- And many more specialty options

### Finding the Right Theme
1. If you are part of a specific ministry group (Kids, Youth, Senoritas), look for the theme designed for your group.
2. If you prefer reduced brightness, try **Dark** or **Dark Blue**.
3. You can change your theme as often as you like -- there is no limit.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Theme does not change after tapping Apply | Close and reopen the app. The theme should take effect on restart. |
| Some screens still show the old theme | Make sure you are running the latest version of the app. Update from the App Store or Google Play. |
| I cannot find the Themes option | Go to **Settings** (gear icon) and look for the **Themes** section. It may also be listed under **Appearance**. |
| The theme makes text hard to read | Switch to a different theme. **Light** and **Dark** are designed for the best readability. |
| My theme resets after logging out | Themes are saved to your account. Make sure you are logged in to keep your theme preference. |
