# Troubleshooting

Having trouble with the CGC app? You are in the right place. Browse the guides below to find help with your specific issue, or try the quick fixes at the bottom of this page.

## Troubleshooting Guides

- **[Login Issues](login-issues.md)** -- Cannot log in, forgot password, account locked, social login problems, or session expired.
- **[Playback Issues](playback-issues.md)** -- Audio or video will not play, buffering, no sound, player stuck, or background playback not working.
- **[Download Issues](download-issues.md)** -- Downloads failing, storage full, downloaded content will not play, or downloads disappearing.
- **[Billing Issues](billing-issues.md)** -- Payment declined, subscription not activating, double charges, cancellation problems, or refund requests.

## Quick Fixes to Try First

Before diving into a specific guide, try these general steps that resolve many common issues:

### 1. Update the App
Make sure you are running the latest version of the CGC app.
- **iPhone/iPad:** Open the App Store, search for "CGC," and tap **Update** if available.
- **Android:** Open Google Play, search for "CGC," and tap **Update** if available.

### 2. Check Your Internet Connection
Many features require an active internet connection. Try the following:
- Turn Wi-Fi off and back on.
- Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to see if the issue is network-related.
- Open a web browser and visit any website to confirm your connection is working.

### 3. Restart the App
Close the app completely and reopen it.
- **iPhone:** Swipe up from the bottom of the screen (or double-tap the Home button), find the CGC app, and swipe it up to close.
- **Android:** Tap the recent apps button, find the CGC app, and swipe it away.

### 4. Clear the App Cache
Clearing the cache can resolve display issues and unexpected behavior.
- Go to **Settings** in the CGC app and look for **Clear Cache** or **Storage**.
- On Android, you can also go to device Settings > Apps > CGC > Storage > Clear Cache.

### 5. Restart Your Device
A device restart can fix many issues caused by temporary glitches.

### 6. Log Out and Log Back In
Sometimes re-authenticating resolves account-related problems.
- Go to **Settings** in the CGC app and tap **Log Out**.
- Log back in with your email and password.
