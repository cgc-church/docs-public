# Download Issues

## What You Can Do
- Fix downloads that fail or get stuck
- Free up storage space when your device is full
- Resolve problems with downloaded content that will not play
- Recover downloads that have disappeared
- Troubleshoot sync issues between devices

## How It Works

**Scenario: A download fails partway through**
- **Given** I tap the download button on a sermon
- **When** the download starts but then stops or shows an error
- **Then** I check my internet connection, make sure I have enough storage space, and try the download again

**Scenario: Downloaded content will not play offline**
- **Given** I have downloaded a sermon for offline listening
- **When** I turn off Wi-Fi and try to play it
- **Then** if it does not play, I delete the download and re-download it while connected to Wi-Fi

**Scenario: My device is out of storage**
- **Given** I try to download content and see a "Storage full" message
- **When** I go to my library and delete downloads I no longer need
- **Then** I free up space and can download new content

**Scenario: My downloads have disappeared**
- **Given** I previously downloaded several sermons
- **When** I go to my downloads and they are no longer there
- **Then** I check if I logged out or if the app was updated, and I re-download the content I need

**Scenario: Downloads are not syncing across devices**
- **Given** I downloaded content on my phone
- **When** I open the app on my tablet
- **Then** the downloads do not appear because downloads are stored locally on each device and must be downloaded separately

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Fixing a Failed Download
1. Check your internet connection. Downloads require a stable connection.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Make sure you have enough storage space on your device.
   - **iPhone:** Go to device Settings > General > iPhone Storage.
   - **Android:** Go to device Settings > Storage.
3. Go back to the content you want to download and tap the download button again.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
4. If the download fails again, try connecting to a different Wi-Fi network.
5. If it still fails, restart the app and try one more time.

### Freeing Up Storage Space
1. Open the CGC app and go to **My Library** or **Downloads**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Look for content you no longer need offline.
3. Swipe left on the item (or tap the options menu) and select **Delete Download**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
4. Repeat for any other downloads you no longer need.
5. You can also free up space on your device by removing other apps, photos, or files you no longer need.

### Fixing Downloaded Content That Will Not Play
1. Go to **My Library** or **Downloads** in the app.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Find the content that will not play.
3. Delete the download by swiping left or tapping the options menu and selecting **Delete Download**.
4. Make sure you are connected to Wi-Fi.
5. Re-download the content by tapping the download button again.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
6. Wait for the download to complete fully (look for a checkmark or completion indicator).
7. Try playing the content again.

### Recovering Disappeared Downloads
1. Downloads can disappear if:
   - You logged out of the app
   - The app was uninstalled and reinstalled
   - A major app update cleared the download cache
2. To recover, simply re-download the content:
   - Browse to the content you want.
   - Tap the download button.
   - Wait for the download to complete.
3. To prevent this in the future, keep the app updated and avoid clearing app data.

### Understanding Downloads Across Devices
1. Downloads are stored locally on each device. They do not sync between devices.
2. If you download a sermon on your phone, it will not automatically appear on your tablet.
3. To have content available on multiple devices, you need to download it separately on each one.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Download stuck at a percentage | Cancel the download and try again. Make sure you have a strong internet connection. |
| "Storage full" error | Free up space by deleting old downloads from the app or removing other files from your device. |
| Downloaded content shows an error when playing | Delete the download and re-download it. The file may have been corrupted during download. |
| Downloads are very slow | Switch to a faster Wi-Fi network. Avoid downloading while streaming or using other bandwidth-heavy apps. |
| All my downloads disappeared after an update | App updates occasionally clear the download cache. Re-download the content you need. |
| I cannot find my downloads | Go to **My Library** or **Downloads** in the app. Make sure you are logged into the same account you used when downloading. |
