# Billing Issues

## What You Can Do
- Resolve declined payment methods
- Fix a subscription that is not activating
- Address double charges or unexpected charges
- Cancel a subscription
- Request a refund

## How It Works

**Scenario: My payment was declined**
- **Given** I am trying to subscribe or renew my subscription
- **When** my payment is declined
- **Then** I check that my card details are correct, the card has not expired, and there are sufficient funds, then try again

**Scenario: My subscription is not activating after payment**
- **Given** I completed a payment for a subscription
- **When** my account still shows as a free account
- **Then** I log out and log back in to refresh my account status, or contact support if the issue persists

**Scenario: I see a double charge on my statement**
- **Given** I notice two charges from CGC on my bank or card statement
- **When** I check my subscription history in the app
- **Then** I compare the charges with my subscription dates and contact support if the charges are incorrect

**Scenario: I want to cancel my subscription**
- **Given** I no longer want to continue my subscription
- **When** I go to **Settings > Subscription** and tap **Cancel**
- **Then** my subscription stops renewing at the end of the current billing period

**Scenario: I want to request a refund**
- **Given** I was charged incorrectly or want a refund for a recent payment
- **When** I contact support with my account details and reason for the refund
- **Then** the support team reviews my request and processes a refund if eligible

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Fixing a Declined Payment
1. Open the CGC app and go to **Settings > Subscription** or **Account > Payment**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Check your saved payment method:
   - Make sure the card number is correct.
   - Make sure the expiration date has not passed.
   - Make sure the billing address matches what your bank has on file.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. If the card is expired or incorrect, tap **Update Payment Method** and enter a valid card.
4. Try the payment again.
5. If the payment is still declined, contact your bank to make sure they are not blocking the transaction.

### Activating a Subscription After Payment
1. If your payment went through but your subscription is not active:
   - Close the app completely.
   - Reopen the app and log in again.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Go to **Settings > Subscription** to check your subscription status.
3. If it still shows as inactive, try logging out and logging back in.
4. If the issue persists after 24 hours, contact support with your payment confirmation or receipt.

### Checking for Double Charges
1. Open the CGC app and go to **Settings > Subscription**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Review your subscription history and billing dates.
3. Compare the charges on your bank or card statement with the dates shown in the app.
4. Common reasons for what looks like a double charge:
   - A failed payment was retried and both appear on your statement temporarily.
   - You upgraded your plan, resulting in a prorated charge.
   - A pending charge that has not yet cleared.
5. If you believe the charge is incorrect, contact support with the details.

### Canceling Your Subscription
1. Open the CGC app and go to **Settings > Subscription**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Tap **Cancel Subscription** or **Manage Subscription**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation.
4. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.
5. **Important:** If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you may need to cancel through your device's subscription settings:
   - **iPhone:** Go to device Settings > your name > Subscriptions > CGC > Cancel.
   - **Android:** Open Google Play > Menu > Subscriptions > CGC > Cancel.

### Requesting a Refund
1. Contact support at support@christgospel.org.
2. Include the following in your message:
   - Your full name
   - The email address associated with your CGC account
   - The date and amount of the charge
   - The reason for your refund request
3. The support team will review your request and respond within a few business days.
4. If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you may also need to request a refund through Apple or Google:
   - **Apple:** Visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
   - **Google:** Open Google Play > Account > Order History > find the charge > Request a refund.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Payment keeps getting declined | Verify your card details and expiration date. Contact your bank to make sure they are not blocking the charge. Try a different payment method. |
| Subscription shows as active but I cannot access premium content | Log out and log back in. If the issue persists, clear the app cache and try again. Contact support if the problem continues. |
| I was charged after canceling | Check the cancellation date. You may have been charged for the current billing period before the cancellation took effect. If you believe the charge is an error, contact support. |
| I cannot find the cancel button | If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you must cancel through your device's subscription settings, not through the CGC app. |
| My refund has not been processed | Refunds may take 5 to 10 business days to appear on your statement. If it has been longer, contact support with your refund request details. |
