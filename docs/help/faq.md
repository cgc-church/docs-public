# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Login

### What is Christ Gospel Church's digital platform?

A suite of applications that helps church members access sermons, manage subscriptions, and stay connected with the community — available on web and mobile.

### How do I create an account?

Download the mobile app or visit the web platform and follow the registration prompts. You'll need a valid email address. For a detailed walkthrough, see our [Login Guide](/help/login-guide).

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your email. The link expires after 24 hours, so be sure to use it promptly.

### How do I change my password?

In the mobile app, go to **Settings > Account > Change Password**. On the admin dashboard, go to **Profile > Security > Change Password**. You'll need to enter your current password before setting a new one.

### How do I change my email or profile information?

Go to **Settings > Profile** in the app or web dashboard. After changing your email, you'll receive a verification link at your new email address.

### Can I use the same account on multiple devices?

Yes. Your account works across all your devices — you can sign in on your phone, tablet, and computer using the same email and password.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact us at **support@christgospel.org** with a request to delete your account. Please use the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm once completed.

## Subscriptions & Billing

### How do I subscribe?

Visit the [Subscriptions](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) page and choose a plan. You'll be guided through a secure checkout process powered by Stripe. For step-by-step instructions, see our [Subscription Management Guide](/help/manage-subscription).

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings > Subscription** and click "Cancel Plan." Your access continues until the current billing period ends. You won't be charged again unless you resubscribe.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through Stripe.

### Can I share my subscription with family members?

Yes! We offer family plans that let you share your subscription with members of your household. Visit the [Subscriptions](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) page for details, or see our [Subscription Management Guide](/help/manage-subscription#family-plan-details).

### Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed through **Stripe**, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store your full card number on our servers. Your financial data is encrypted and handled entirely by Stripe's secure infrastructure.

### Will I get a receipt for my payments?

Yes. A receipt is automatically sent to your email after each payment. You can also view your billing history in **Settings > Subscription > Billing History**.

## Mobile App

### Is the mobile app free?

The app is free to download. Some premium content, such as sermon downloads for offline listening, may require a subscription.

### What devices are supported?

The app works on **iOS 15.0 or later** and **Android 10.0 (API 29) or later**. It runs on iPhones, iPads, and Android phones and tablets.

### How do I enable dark mode?

Go to **Settings > Appearance** in the app. You can choose between Light, Dark, or System Default (which follows your device's setting).

### How do I change the app language?

The app supports **English** and **Spanish**. Go to **Settings > Language** to switch between languages. The change takes effect immediately.

### Why am I not receiving push notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled for the CGC app in your device settings. On iOS, go to **Settings > Notifications > CGC**. On Android, go to **Settings > Apps > CGC > Notifications**. Also ensure you're logged in to the app.

## Sermons & Content

### How do I find a specific sermon?

You can browse sermons by preacher, topic, or date. You can also use the **Search** feature to find sermons by title or keyword.

### Can I download sermons for offline listening?

Yes, with an active subscription you can download sermons to listen offline. See our [Offline & Downloads Guide](/help/offline-downloads) for details.

### Why can't I download sermons?

Sermon downloads require an active subscription. If you have a subscription and are still unable to download, check your internet connection and make sure your app is up to date. See [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) for more help.

### Can I create playlists of my favorite sermons?

Yes! You can create custom playlists in the mobile app. Tap the three-dot menu on any sermon and select **Add to Playlist**. You can create as many playlists as you like.

### Are new sermons added regularly?

Yes. New sermons are added on a regular basis. Enable push notifications to be alerted when new content is available.

## Technical Issues

### The app isn't loading. What should I do?

See our [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) guide for step-by-step solutions to common app issues.

### What languages are supported?

All applications support **English** and **Spanish**.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication (including optional two-factor verification for administrators), and role-based access controls.

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us at **support@christgospel.org** with a description of the issue, your device and app version, and screenshots if possible. This helps us identify and fix the problem quickly.

### The web dashboard shows a blank screen. What should I do?

Try a hard refresh: press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac). If that doesn't help, clear your browser cache or try a different browser. See [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) for more solutions.
