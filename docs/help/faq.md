# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Christ Gospel Church's digital platform?

A suite of applications that helps church members access sermons, manage subscriptions, and stay connected with the community — available on web and mobile.

### Is the mobile app free?

The app is free to download. Some premium content may require a subscription.

### What languages are supported?

All applications support **English** and **Spanish**.

## Account

### How do I create an account?

Download the mobile app or visit the web platform and follow the registration prompts. You'll need a valid email address.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your email.

### How do I change my email or profile information?

Go to **Settings > Profile** in the app or web dashboard.

## Subscriptions

### How do I subscribe?

Visit the [Subscriptions](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) page and choose a plan.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings > Subscription** and click "Cancel Plan." Your access continues until the current billing period ends.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards through Stripe.

## Technical

### The app isn't loading. What should I do?

See our [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) guide.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication (including optional two-factor verification), and role-based access controls.
