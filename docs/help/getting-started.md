# Getting Started

Welcome to Christ Gospel Church's digital platform! Whether you're here to listen to sermons, stay connected with your church community, or manage your subscription, we're glad you're here.

## What You Can Do

With the CGC platform, you can:

- **Listen to sermons** — Browse a growing library of sermons by preacher, topic, or date, and stream them anytime
- **Download for offline** — Save sermons to your device so you can listen without an internet connection
- **Create playlists** — Organize your favorite sermons into custom playlists
- **Get notified** — Receive push notifications when new content is available
- **Choose your language** — Use the app in English or Spanish
- **Manage your subscription** — Subscribe, update your payment method, and view your billing history

## Step 1: Download the Mobile App

The CGC app is available for both iOS and Android:

- **iPhone / iPad**: Download from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com)
- **Android**: Download from [Google Play](https://play.google.com)

The app is free to download. A subscription unlocks premium features like offline downloads.

## Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Open the CGC app after downloading it
2. Tap **Create Account**
3. Enter your **name**, **email address**, and choose a **password**
4. Check your email and tap the **verification link** to confirm your account
5. Return to the app and **sign in**

For a detailed walkthrough, including admin accounts and two-factor authentication, see the [Login Guide](/help/login-guide).

## Step 3: Start Exploring

Once you're signed in, here are some things to try:

- **Browse sermons**: Head to the sermon library and explore by preacher, topic, or date
- **Stream a sermon**: Tap any sermon to start listening right away
- **Set up your profile**: Go to **Settings > Profile** to customize your account
- **Subscribe**: Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org) to unlock premium features like offline downloads and family sharing

## For Church Administrators

If you're a church administrator, you also have access to the **Admin Dashboard**:

1. Visit [admin.christgospel.org](https://admin.christgospel.org)
2. Sign in with your administrator credentials
3. If prompted, enter the verification code sent to your device
4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard where you can manage sermons, users, and settings

Admin accounts are created by your church's admin team. Contact **support@christgospel.org** if you need admin access.
