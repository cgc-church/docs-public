# Subscription Management Guide

This guide covers everything you need to know about managing your CGC subscription, from signing up to updating your payment information.

## How to Subscribe

1. Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org)
2. Browse the available plans and select the one that's right for you
3. Click **Subscribe** on your chosen plan
4. If you're not already signed in, you'll be asked to **log in** or **create an account**
5. You'll be directed to a secure **Stripe checkout** page
6. Enter your **payment information** (credit or debit card)
7. Review your order and click **Subscribe**
8. You'll receive a **confirmation email** with your subscription details
9. Your premium features are now active — enjoy!

::: tip
All payment processing is handled by **Stripe**, a trusted and PCI-compliant payment platform. Your card information is never stored on our servers.
:::

## How to View Billing History

1. Open the CGC app or visit the web dashboard
2. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
3. Tap or click **Billing History**
4. You'll see a list of all past payments, including dates, amounts, and receipt links
5. Tap any entry to view or download the receipt

Receipts are also sent to your email after each payment.

## How to Update Your Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Tap or click **Payment Method**
3. Click **Update Card**
4. Enter your new card details on the secure Stripe form
5. Click **Save** to confirm the change

Your new card will be used for all future payments. The update takes effect immediately.

## How to Cancel Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Tap or click **Cancel Plan**
3. You'll be asked to confirm — select **Yes, Cancel**
4. Your subscription remains active until the **end of your current billing period**
5. You won't be charged again unless you choose to resubscribe

::: info
After cancellation, you'll keep access to your subscription benefits until the billing period ends. After that, you'll return to the free tier and will no longer be able to download content for offline use.
:::

## How to Resubscribe After Cancellation

If you've previously cancelled and want to come back:

1. Visit [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org)
2. Sign in with your existing account
3. Choose a plan and complete the checkout process
4. Your premium access will be restored immediately

Your account history, playlists, and preferences are preserved — you'll pick up right where you left off.

## Family Plan Details

Family plans allow you to share your subscription with members of your household:

- **What's included**: A family plan covers one primary account holder and up to **4 additional family members**
- **How it works**: Each family member gets their own individual account with their own playlists and preferences
- **How to set up**: Subscribe to the Family plan at [subscriptions.christgospel.org](https://subscriptions.christgospel.org), then go to **Settings > Subscription > Family Members** to invite your family by email
- **Managing members**: The primary account holder can add or remove family members at any time from the subscription settings

::: tip
Each family member needs their own CGC account. When you invite them, they'll receive an email to sign in or create an account.
:::

## Refund Policy

- Refund requests can be made within **7 days** of a payment
- To request a refund, contact **support@christgospel.org** with your account email and the reason for the request
- Refunds are processed back to your original payment method and typically appear within **5-10 business days**
- If you cancel your subscription mid-cycle, you will not be charged again, but the current period is not refunded — you retain access until it ends

## Need Help?

For any billing questions or subscription issues, contact us at **support@christgospel.org**. Please include the email address associated with your account so we can assist you quickly.
