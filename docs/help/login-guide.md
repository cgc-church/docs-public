# Login Guide

This guide walks you through creating an account, logging in, resetting your password, and resolving common login issues.

## Creating an Account

### Mobile App

1. Download the CGC app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com) (iOS) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com) (Android)
2. Open the app and tap **Create Account**
3. Enter your **full name**, **email address**, and choose a **password**
4. You will receive a verification email — open it and tap the **Verify Email** link
5. Return to the app and sign in with your new credentials
6. You're all set! You can now browse sermons, explore content, and set up your profile

### Admin Dashboard

Administrator accounts are created by your church's admin team. If you need admin access:

1. Contact your church administrator or email **support@christgospel.org**
2. Once your account is created, you'll receive an invitation email
3. Click the link in the email to set your password
4. Visit the [Admin Dashboard](https://admin.christgospel.org) and sign in
5. You may be asked to set up two-factor authentication on your first login (see below)

## How to Log In

### Mobile App

1. Open the CGC app on your device
2. Tap **Sign In**
3. Enter your **email address** and **password**
4. Tap **Sign In** — you'll be taken to the home screen

### Admin Dashboard

1. Visit [admin.christgospel.org](https://admin.christgospel.org)
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**
4. If you have two-factor authentication enabled, enter the verification code sent to your email or device
5. You'll be redirected to the dashboard home screen

## How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it in a few steps:

1. On the login screen, tap or click **Forgot Password**
2. Enter the **email address** associated with your account
3. Check your email for a password reset link (be sure to check your spam/junk folder)
4. Click the link and enter a **new password**
5. Return to the app or dashboard and sign in with your new password

::: tip
The password reset link expires after **24 hours**. If it has expired, simply request a new one.
:::

## Two-Factor Authentication (Admin Users)

For added security, church administrators can enable two-factor authentication (2FA):

### Setting Up 2FA

1. Sign in to the [Admin Dashboard](https://admin.christgospel.org)
2. Go to **Profile > Security**
3. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
4. Choose your verification method — **email** or **authenticator app**
5. Follow the prompts to complete setup
6. Save your **backup codes** in a safe place — these can be used if you lose access to your verification method

### Using 2FA

1. Sign in with your email and password as usual
2. When prompted, enter the **verification code** from your email or authenticator app
3. Click **Verify** to complete the login

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### "Invalid email or password" error

- Double-check that your email address is spelled correctly
- Make sure Caps Lock is not turned on
- Try resetting your password using the steps above

### Verification email not received

- Check your **spam or junk** folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes — emails can sometimes take a moment to arrive
- If you still haven't received it, tap **Resend Verification Email** on the login screen

### Account locked

If you've entered an incorrect password too many times, your account may be temporarily locked for security. Wait **15 minutes** and try again, or reset your password.

### Two-factor code not working

- Make sure you're entering the most recent code (codes expire quickly)
- Check that your device's clock is set to the correct time
- Try using one of your **backup codes**
- If you've lost access to your verification method, contact **support@christgospel.org**

## Need More Help?

If you're still having trouble logging in, reach out to us at **support@christgospel.org** and we'll be happy to help.
