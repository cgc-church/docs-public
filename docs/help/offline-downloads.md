# Offline & Downloads Guide

With an active subscription, you can download sermons to listen or watch anytime — even without an internet connection. This guide explains how to download content, manage your downloads, and troubleshoot common issues.

## How to Download Sermons

1. Open the CGC app on your mobile device
2. Browse or search for the sermon you'd like to download
3. Tap the **Download** button (the down arrow icon) on the sermon
4. The download will begin — you'll see a progress indicator
5. Once complete, the sermon will appear in your **Downloads** section

You can continue using the app while content downloads in the background.

::: info
Downloading sermons for offline access requires an active subscription. If you're on the free tier, you'll be prompted to subscribe when you tap the download button.
:::

## Accessing Downloaded Content

1. Open the CGC app
2. Go to the **Downloads** section from the main menu or bottom navigation
3. You'll see all your downloaded sermons listed
4. Tap any sermon to start listening or watching — no internet connection needed

Downloaded sermons are available as long as your subscription is active.

## Managing Downloaded Content

### Viewing Download Size

Each downloaded sermon shows its file size. To see how much total storage your downloads are using:

1. Go to **Settings > Storage**
2. You'll see the total space used by downloaded content

### Storage Requirements

Download sizes vary depending on the content type:

| Content Type | Approximate Size |
|---|---|
| Audio sermon (30 min) | 15 - 25 MB |
| Audio sermon (60 min) | 30 - 50 MB |
| Video sermon (30 min) | 150 - 300 MB |
| Video sermon (60 min) | 300 - 600 MB |

::: tip
If storage space is a concern, consider downloading audio-only versions of sermons. They use significantly less space than video downloads.
:::

## How to Delete Downloads

### Deleting a Single Download

1. Go to your **Downloads** section
2. Swipe left on the sermon you want to remove (iOS) or long-press and select **Delete** (Android)
3. Confirm the deletion

### Deleting All Downloads

1. Go to **Settings > Storage**
2. Tap **Clear All Downloads**
3. Confirm — all downloaded content will be removed from your device

Deleting a download only removes it from your device. You can always download it again later.

## Download Settings

You can customize how downloads work in **Settings > Downloads**:

- **Download over Wi-Fi only**: When enabled, downloads will only start when you're connected to Wi-Fi. This helps avoid using your mobile data. Enabled by default.
- **Audio quality**: Choose between Standard and High quality for audio downloads.
- **Video quality**: Choose between Standard (SD) and High (HD) for video downloads. Higher quality means larger file sizes.

## Troubleshooting Download Issues

### Download won't start

- Check your **internet connection** — downloads require an active connection to begin
- Make sure you have an **active subscription**
- Check that you have enough **storage space** on your device
- If "Wi-Fi only" is enabled, make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi

### Download is stuck or very slow

- Check your internet connection speed
- Pause and resume the download by tapping the progress indicator
- If the issue persists, cancel the download and try again
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data (if "Wi-Fi only" is disabled)

### Downloaded content won't play

- Make sure the download completed successfully (look for a checkmark icon)
- Try deleting the download and re-downloading it
- Update the app to the latest version
- Restart the app

### Downloads disappeared

- Downloads are tied to your account — make sure you're signed in
- If your subscription has expired, downloaded content may become unavailable until you resubscribe
- If you recently updated or reinstalled the app, you may need to re-download your content
