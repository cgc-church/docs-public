# Videos

## What You Can Do
- Browse the full video library
- Watch videos directly in the app
- Switch to fullscreen mode for an immersive viewing experience
- Use the mini player to keep watching while browsing
- Browse videos by category
- Search for specific videos

## How It Works

**Scenario: Browsing the video library**
- **Given** I am on the Videos screen
- **When** I scroll through the video list
- **Then** I see videos displayed with their thumbnail, title, and category

**Scenario: Watching a video**
- **Given** I am on the Videos screen
- **When** I tap on a video thumbnail or title
- **Then** the video opens and begins to play

**Scenario: Switching to fullscreen**
- **Given** I am watching a video
- **When** I tap the **Fullscreen** icon (or rotate my device to landscape)
- **Then** the video expands to fill the entire screen

**Scenario: Using the mini player**
- **Given** I am watching a video
- **When** I swipe down on the video or tap the **Minimize** icon
- **Then** the video shrinks to a mini player in the corner and I can continue browsing the app

**Scenario: Browsing by category**
- **Given** I am on the Videos screen
- **When** I tap on a category (for example, "Worship," "Teaching," or "Testimonies")
- **Then** only videos in that category are displayed

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Video won't play | Check your internet connection. Videos require a stable connection to stream. Try refreshing the page. |
| Video is buffering or stuttering | You may be on a slow connection. Try connecting to Wi-Fi or moving closer to your router. |
| No sound on video | Make sure your device is not on silent mode. Check that the volume is turned up. |
| Fullscreen won't activate | Tap directly on the fullscreen icon in the player controls. If using rotation, make sure your device's auto-rotate is enabled. |
| Mini player disappeared | The mini player may have been closed. Go back to the Videos section and tap the video again to resume. |
| Can't find a specific video | Try different search terms. Browse by category if you know the type of video you are looking for. |
