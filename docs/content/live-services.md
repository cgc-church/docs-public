# Live Services

## What You Can Do
- Watch live church services as they happen
- View the schedule of upcoming live services
- Get notified when a live service is about to start
- Watch recordings of past services you may have missed

## How It Works

**Scenario: Watching a live service**
- **Given** a live service is currently streaming
- **When** I open the Live Services screen and tap **Watch Live**
- **Then** the live video stream begins playing

**Scenario: Viewing the service schedule**
- **Given** I am on the Live Services screen
- **When** I look at the schedule section
- **Then** I see the dates and times of upcoming live services

**Scenario: Turning on notifications for live services**
- **Given** I am on the Live Services screen
- **When** I tap **Notify Me** or enable live service notifications
- **Then** I will receive a notification on my device when a live service is about to begin

**Scenario: Watching an archived service**
- **Given** I am on the Live Services screen
- **When** I scroll to the past services section and tap on a previous service
- **Then** the recorded service begins to play

**Scenario: Arriving when no service is live**
- **Given** no live service is currently streaming
- **When** I open the Live Services screen
- **Then** I see the next scheduled service time and the option to browse past recordings

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "No live service" message | Check the schedule to see when the next service is. Live services are only available at their scheduled times. |
| Live stream won't load | Check your internet connection. Live streams require a stable, reasonably fast connection. Try refreshing the page. |
| Stream is buffering or lagging | You may be on a slow connection. Try switching to Wi-Fi or moving closer to your router. Close other apps that may be using bandwidth. |
| Did not receive a notification | Make sure notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings. Also verify that the **Notify Me** option is turned on in the app. |
| Can't find a past service recording | Not all services may be archived. Check the Past Services section and try scrolling further back. Some recordings may take time to be posted after the service ends. |
| Video has no sound | Make sure your device is not on silent mode and the volume is turned up. Check headphone or Bluetooth connections. |
