# Books

## What You Can Do
- Browse the full book catalog
- Read books directly in the app
- Highlight text passages
- Add personal notes to any page
- Bookmark pages to return to later
- Adjust font size, font style, and display settings in the reader

## How It Works

**Scenario: Browsing the book catalog**
- **Given** I am on the Books screen
- **When** I scroll through the catalog
- **Then** I see available books with their cover image, title, and author

**Scenario: Reading a book**
- **Given** I am viewing a book's detail page
- **When** I tap **Read**
- **Then** the book opens in the reader and I can swipe or tap to turn pages

**Scenario: Highlighting text**
- **Given** I am reading a book in the reader
- **When** I long-press on a word and drag to select a passage, then tap **Highlight**
- **Then** the selected text is highlighted and saved

**Scenario: Adding a note**
- **Given** I have selected text in the reader
- **When** I tap **Add Note** and type my note, then tap **Save**
- **Then** my note is attached to that passage and a note icon appears in the margin

**Scenario: Bookmarking a page**
- **Given** I am reading a book
- **When** I tap the **Bookmark** icon in the top corner of the page
- **Then** the page is bookmarked and I can return to it later from the bookmarks list

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Browsing the Book Catalog
1. Open the app and tap **Books** from the main menu or home screen.

2. Scroll through the catalog to see available books. Each book shows its cover, title, and author.

3. Use the search bar or category filters to find specific books.

### Reading a Book
1. Tap on a book cover or title to open its detail page.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. Tap the **Read** button to open the book in the reader.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. Swipe left or right (or tap the edges of the screen) to turn pages.
4. Your reading progress is saved automatically. When you return to the book, it opens where you left off.

### Highlighting Text
1. While reading, long-press on a word to start a selection.
2. Drag the selection handles to cover the passage you want to highlight.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. A menu appears above the selection. Tap **Highlight**.
4. Choose a highlight color if options are available.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
5. To view all your highlights, open the book menu and tap **Highlights**.

### Adding Notes
1. Select text in the reader by long-pressing and dragging.
2. Tap **Add Note** from the menu that appears.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. Type your note in the text box.
4. Tap **Save**. A small note icon appears in the margin next to the passage.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
5. To view all your notes, open the book menu and tap **Notes**.

### Bookmarking Pages
1. While reading, tap the **Bookmark** icon in the top corner of the screen.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
2. The icon fills in to confirm the page is bookmarked.
3. To see all your bookmarks, open the book menu and tap **Bookmarks**.
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
4. Tap any bookmark in the list to jump directly to that page.

### Adjusting Reader Settings
1. While reading, tap the center of the screen to show the reader toolbar.
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (usually an "Aa" icon).
<!-- TODO: Screenshot -->
3. Adjust the following settings:
   - **Font size** -- Use the slider or plus/minus buttons to make text larger or smaller.
   - **Font style** -- Choose from available font options.
   - **Background color** -- Switch between light, sepia, or dark reading modes.
4. Changes apply immediately. Close the settings panel to continue reading.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Book won't open | Check your internet connection. The book may need to download before it can be read. |
| Lost my reading progress | Make sure you are signed in to your account. Reading progress syncs with your account. |
| Highlights or notes disappeared | Verify you are signed in to the same account. Your annotations are saved to your profile. |
| Text is too small or too large | Use the reader settings (tap the "Aa" icon) to adjust font size. |
| Pages are hard to read | Try switching to a different background mode (light, sepia, or dark) in reader settings. |
| Can't select text to highlight | Long-press firmly on a word and wait for the selection handles to appear before dragging. |
