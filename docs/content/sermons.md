# Sermons

## What You Can Do
- Browse the full sermon archive
- Filter sermons by preacher, date, topic, or scripture reference
- Play sermons directly in the app
- Download sermons for offline listening
- View sermon details including date preached, preacher name, scripture references, and topic tags
- Share sermons with others

## How It Works

**Scenario: Browsing the sermon archive**
- **Given** I am on the Sermons screen
- **When** I scroll through the sermon list
- **Then** I see sermons displayed with their title, preacher name, and date

**Scenario: Filtering sermons by preacher**
- **Given** I am on the Sermons screen
- **When** I tap the filter icon and select a preacher from the list
- **Then** only sermons by that preacher are shown

**Scenario: Searching sermons by topic**
- **Given** I am on the Sermons screen
- **When** I tap the search bar and type a topic (for example, "faith" or "prayer")
- **Then** sermons matching that topic appear in the results

**Scenario: Playing a sermon**
- **Given** I am viewing a sermon in the list
- **When** I tap on the sermon title
- **Then** the sermon detail screen opens and the sermon begins to play

**Scenario: Downloading a sermon for offline listening**
- **Given** I am on a sermon's detail screen
- **When** I tap the download icon
- **Then** the sermon is saved to my device and available without an internet connection

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Browsing Sermons
1. Open the app and tap **Sermons** from the main menu or home screen.

### Filtering Sermons
1. On the Sermons screen, tap the **Filter** icon (usually at the top of the screen).

2. Choose your filter criteria:
   - **Preacher** -- Select a specific preacher from the dropdown.
   - **Date** -- Pick a date range to narrow results.
   - **Topic** -- Choose a topic category.
3. Tap **Apply** to see the filtered results.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Sermon won't play | Check your internet connection. If you are offline, make sure the sermon has been downloaded first. |
| Filter not showing results | Try broadening your filter criteria. Clear all filters and start with just one. |
| Download stuck or failed | Make sure you have enough storage space on your device and a stable internet connection. Try again. |
| Audio cuts out during playback | Check that your device is not on silent mode. Try closing other apps that may be using audio. |
| Can't find a specific sermon | Use the search bar and try different keywords such as the preacher's name, a scripture reference, or a topic. |
