# Quote of the Day

## What You Can Do
- See a new inspirational quote each day on your home screen
- Bookmark your favorite quotes to revisit later
- Share quotes with friends and family through messaging apps, social media, or email

## How It Works

**Scenario: Viewing the daily quote**
- **Given** I open the app
- **When** I look at the home screen
- **Then** I see today's Quote of the Day displayed prominently

**Scenario: Bookmarking a quote**
- **Given** I am viewing the Quote of the Day on the home screen
- **When** I tap the **Bookmark** icon on the quote
- **Then** the quote is saved to my bookmarked quotes list

**Scenario: Sharing a quote**
- **Given** I am viewing the Quote of the Day
- **When** I tap the **Share** icon on the quote
- **Then** my device's share menu opens and I can send the quote through messaging, social media, email, or other apps

**Scenario: Viewing bookmarked quotes**
- **Given** I have bookmarked one or more quotes
- **When** I go to my bookmarked quotes list
- **Then** I see all the quotes I have previously saved

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Quote of the Day is not showing | Make sure you have an internet connection. The daily quote may need to load from the server. Try pulling down to refresh the home screen. |
| Same quote showing as yesterday | The quote updates once per day. If it has not changed, try closing and reopening the app. Check that your device's date and time are set correctly. |
| Share menu not appearing | Make sure you are tapping the Share icon on the quote card. If the menu still does not appear, try restarting the app. |
| Bookmarked quote disappeared | Verify you are signed in to your account. Bookmarks are saved to your profile and require you to be logged in. |
| Shared quote looks wrong in the message | Some messaging apps may format the shared text differently. Try using "Copy to clipboard" and pasting the quote manually. |
