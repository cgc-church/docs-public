# Podcasts & Radio

## What You Can Do
- Listen to CGC Radio live streams
- Browse available podcast shows
- Play individual podcast episodes
- Listen to audio in the background while using other apps
- See what is currently playing on CGC Radio

## How It Works

**Scenario: Listening to CGC Radio live**
- **Given** I am on the Radio screen
- **When** I tap the **Play** button on the CGC Radio live stream
- **Then** the live radio stream begins playing

**Scenario: Browsing podcast shows**
- **Given** I am on the Podcasts screen
- **When** I scroll through the list of available podcast shows
- **Then** I see each show with its name, description, and artwork

**Scenario: Playing a podcast episode**
- **Given** I am viewing a podcast show's episode list
- **When** I tap on an episode
- **Then** the episode begins to play and playback controls appear

**Scenario: Listening in the background**
- **Given** audio (radio or a podcast) is currently playing
- **When** I switch to another app or lock my device
- **Then** the audio continues to play in the background

**Scenario: Checking what is on CGC Radio right now**
- **Given** I am on the Radio screen
- **When** I look at the Now Playing area
- **Then** I see the name of the current program or show that is airing live

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Radio stream won't play | Check your internet connection. Live streams require a stable connection. Try tapping Play again. |
| Audio stops when I leave the app | Make sure background audio is allowed for the app in your device settings. On some devices, battery optimization can stop background playback -- add the app to your battery exceptions. |
| Podcast episode won't load | Check your internet connection. The episode may take a moment to buffer. Try again after a few seconds. |
| No sound | Ensure your device is not on silent mode and the volume is turned up. Check that headphones or Bluetooth speakers are connected properly if you are using them. |
| Can't find a specific podcast or episode | Use the search feature on the Podcasts screen. Try searching by show name, episode title, or topic. |
